Development Process FAQs

Learn about our development process, project management approach, and how we work with UK businesses.

What is your development process?

Our development process follows a structured approach: Discovery and consultation to understand your needs, Design and wireframing to visualize the solution, Development and coding to build your bespoke solution, Testing and quality assurance to ensure everything works perfectly, Deployment and launch, and Ongoing support. We maintain transparent communication throughout, with regular updates and opportunities for feedback at each stage.

How do you manage projects?

We use agile project management methodologies with clear milestones, regular check-ins, and transparent progress tracking. Each project has a dedicated project manager who coordinates communication, tracks progress, manages timelines, and ensures quality. We use project management tools to keep you informed and provide visibility into development progress throughout the project lifecycle.

Will we be involved during development?

Yes, we believe in collaborative development. You'll be involved at key stages including design reviews, feature demonstrations, testing phases, and decision points. We schedule regular check-ins (typically weekly or bi-weekly) to share progress, gather feedback, and ensure the project aligns with your vision. Your input is valuable and helps us deliver exactly what you need.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

We have structured feedback processes at each project stage. During design phases, we incorporate revisions based on your input. During development, we have dedicated feedback windows where you can review work and request adjustments. We track all feedback systematically and ensure changes are implemented efficiently while maintaining project timelines. Clear communication channels ensure your feedback is always heard and addressed.

What happens if we need changes?

We understand that requirements can evolve. If you need changes during development, we assess the impact on timeline and budget, then discuss options transparently. Minor adjustments within scope are accommodated naturally. For significant changes, we provide updated timelines and pricing before proceeding, ensuring you can make informed decisions. Our flexible approach allows us to adapt while maintaining project quality.

How do you ensure quality?

Quality assurance is integrated throughout our process. We conduct code reviews, automated testing, cross-browser compatibility testing, performance optimization, security audits, accessibility checks, and user acceptance testing. Our quality standards ensure your solution is robust, secure, performant, and user-friendly. We don't launch until everything meets our quality benchmarks.

Do you provide project timelines?

Yes, we provide detailed project timelines at the start of each project, breaking down work into phases with specific milestones and deliverables. Timelines include design, development, testing, and deployment phases with realistic estimates. We update timelines if changes occur and communicate any adjustments promptly. Clear timelines help manage expectations and ensure everyone stays aligned.

How do you communicate progress?

We maintain regular communication through scheduled check-ins, progress reports, demo sessions, and project management dashboards where you can see real-time updates. We prefer weekly or bi-weekly meetings depending on project phase, supplemented by email updates and shared documentation. Our communication style is clear, proactive, and ensures you're always informed about project status.

What happens after launch?

After launch, we provide post-launch support including bug fixes, performance monitoring, user training, documentation handover, and assistance with any immediate issues. We offer ongoing maintenance packages for continued support, updates, and enhancements. The relationship continues beyond launch—we're committed to your long-term success and can help evolve your solution as your business grows.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely. We excel at collaborating with internal teams, understanding existing workflows, and integrating our work seamlessly with your operations. We can work alongside your developers, designers, or project managers, adapting our processes to complement your team structure. Effective collaboration ensures smooth integration and knowledge transfer.

Ready to start your project?

Let's discuss your project and how our process can work for you.
