Pricing & Payment FAQs

Transparent answers about web development costs, pricing structure, and payment options for UK businesses.

How much does bespoke web development cost?

Bespoke web development costs vary based on project complexity and requirements. Simple business websites typically start from £2,500, while more complex platforms with advanced features can range from £5,000 to £30,000+. Enterprise-level solutions may exceed this range. We provide transparent, upfront quotes based on your specific needs after an initial consultation. Our pricing reflects the quality, customization, and ongoing value we deliver.

What factors affect pricing?

Several factors influence pricing: project complexity, number of pages and features, custom functionality requirements, design complexity, integration needs, content management requirements, e-commerce capabilities, third-party integrations, timeline urgency, and ongoing maintenance needs. We assess all factors during consultation to provide accurate, transparent pricing.

Do you offer fixed-price quotes?

Yes, we provide fixed-price quotes for projects with clearly defined scope and requirements. After understanding your needs, we prepare detailed proposals with fixed pricing, ensuring no surprises. For projects where requirements may evolve, we can work on a flexible pricing model with clear milestones and transparent change management processes.

What payment options are available?

We offer flexible payment options including upfront payment, milestone-based payments (typically 50% upfront, 25% at milestone, 25% on completion), and payment plans for larger projects. We accept bank transfers, BACS, and other standard payment methods. Payment terms are discussed and agreed upon before project commencement.

Are there any hidden costs?

No, we believe in complete transparency. All costs are clearly outlined in our proposals before project start. The only additional costs would be for scope changes you request after project approval, which we discuss and agree upon before implementation. We never charge hidden fees or surprise costs.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer payment plans for larger projects, typically structured across project milestones. This helps manage cash flow while ensuring your project progresses smoothly. Payment plan terms are customized based on project duration and value, and are agreed upon before work begins.

What's included in the starting price?

Our starting price typically includes custom design, responsive development, basic content management setup, SEO fundamentals, security implementation, hosting setup guidance, basic training, and one round of revisions. Additional features like e-commerce, advanced integrations, custom functionality, and ongoing maintenance are quoted separately based on your needs.

Can you work within our budget?

We work with businesses of all sizes and can often adapt solutions to fit different budgets. We discuss your budget during consultation and can propose phased approaches, prioritize features, or suggest alternatives that deliver maximum value within your constraints. Our goal is finding solutions that work for your business while maintaining quality.

Do you charge for revisions?

We include a reasonable number of revision rounds in our project pricing. Typically, this covers design refinements and minor functionality adjustments during development. Major scope changes or extensive revisions requested after project completion may incur additional costs, which we discuss transparently before proceeding.

What happens if requirements change?

If requirements change during development, we assess the impact and provide updated timelines and pricing before proceeding. We believe in transparent communication and ensure you understand any cost implications. Our change management process is clear and collaborative, ensuring changes are implemented efficiently while maintaining project timelines.
