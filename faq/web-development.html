Web Development FAQs

Find answers to common questions about bespoke web development, custom websites, and our web development services for UK businesses.

What is bespoke web development?

Bespoke web development involves creating custom-built websites and web applications from scratch, tailored specifically to your business needs. Unlike template-based solutions, bespoke development gives you complete control over design, functionality, and features, ensuring your website is unique to your brand and perfectly aligned with your business objectives.

How long does custom web development take?

The timeline for custom web development varies depending on project complexity. A simple business website typically takes 4-8 weeks, while more complex platforms with advanced features can take 12-20 weeks or longer. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation, accounting for design, development, testing, and deployment phases.

What's included in a bespoke website?

A bespoke website includes custom design tailored to your brand, responsive development for all devices, content management capabilities, SEO optimization, security features, hosting setup, and basic training. We can also include e-commerce functionality, integrations with third-party services, analytics setup, and ongoing maintenance packages.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, we specialize in integrating bespoke websites with existing business systems including CRMs, payment gateways, inventory management systems, email marketing platforms, and custom APIs. We ensure seamless data flow between your website and existing infrastructure.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Vue.js), PHP, Python, Node.js, and various databases. We select technologies based on your specific requirements, ensuring optimal performance, security, and scalability for your project.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages that include security updates, bug fixes, performance monitoring, backup management, and content updates. Maintenance packages are customizable to your needs and budget, ensuring your website remains secure and up-to-date.

How is custom development different from templates?

Custom development creates unique solutions built specifically for your business, offering unlimited flexibility and scalability. Templates provide limited customization and may not fully meet your needs. Bespoke development ensures your website is exclusively yours, optimized for your workflows, and free from template constraints.

Can you redesign our existing website?

Absolutely. We can redesign your existing website while preserving important content and functionality, or completely rebuild it from the ground up. We analyze your current site, identify areas for improvement, and create a modern, user-friendly redesign that better serves your business goals.

What's the difference between web development and web design?

Web design focuses on the visual appearance, user interface, and user experience of a website. Web development involves building the functional aspects, writing code, implementing features, and making the design work. We offer both services, ensuring seamless integration between design and functionality.

Do you offer hosting services?

We can recommend and set up hosting solutions tailored to your needs, or work with your existing hosting provider. We help choose the right hosting plan based on your traffic expectations, security requirements, and budget, ensuring optimal performance for your bespoke website.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help. Get in touch for expert guidance on your web development project.
