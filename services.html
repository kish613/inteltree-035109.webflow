Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical website takes 4-8 weeks, while more complex applications can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing options including fixed-price projects, monthly retainers, and hourly rates. Each project gets a custom quote based on your specific requirements and goals.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages to keep your solution running smoothly. This includes updates, security patches, performance optimization, and feature enhancements.

Can you work with our existing tech stack?

Absolutely! Our team has experience with a wide range of technologies and can seamlessly integrate with your existing systems. We adapt to your needs rather than forcing you to change platforms.

What makes IntelTree different from other agencies?

We combine technical excellence with business strategy. Our team doesn't just build software – we partner with you to solve real business problems and drive measurable results. Plus, we're obsessed with quality and user experience.
