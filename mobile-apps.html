Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about mobile app development

How long does mobile app development take?

Timeline varies based on complexity and features. A simple app typically takes 8-12 weeks, while more complex applications with advanced features can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase. Of course, we can accommodate tighter deadlines—even as short as a week for urgent projects—though expedited timelines typically incur an additional fee.

Do you build for both iOS and Android?

Yes! We develop native apps for both platforms, as well as cross-platform solutions using React Native or Flutter. We'll recommend the best approach based on your requirements, budget, and timeline.

What about ongoing maintenance and updates?

We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages to keep your app running smoothly. This includes bug fixes, security updates, OS compatibility updates, performance optimization, and feature enhancements.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating mobile apps with existing backends, APIs, databases, and third-party services. Our team has experience with a wide range of technologies and can seamlessly connect your app to your current infrastructure.
